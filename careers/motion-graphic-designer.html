Motion & Graphic Designer

Kickstart your motion and graphic design career with an internship at Nivaro Digital.

3 months

Paid Internship

On-Site, Noida, Sector 62

Position Overview

Are you passionate about visual storytelling, animation, and creating captivating motion content? Nivaro Digital is seeking a skilled Motion & Graphic Designer who is eager to bring ideas to life through animation, motion graphics, and visual design. In this internship, you’ll have the opportunity to learn from industry experts, contribute directly to client projects, and create visually engaging designs that resonate across digital platforms.

What You’ll Do

As a Motion & Graphic Designer at Nivaro, you will:

  • Conceptualize and produce high-quality motion graphics, 2D animations, and visual designs for digital marketing campaigns, social media, websites, and interactive projects.
  • Collaborate closely with senior designers and marketers to create compelling visual stories through motion design.
  • Develop detailed storyboards, style frames, and design assets that align with brand guidelines and project goals.
  • Apply best practices in animation principles, typography, visual composition, and color theory.
  • Stay updated on the latest design trends, motion techniques, and software tools to keep creative output fresh and innovative.
  • Actively contribute to brainstorming sessions and present creative concepts internally.

What We’re Looking For

Essential:

  • A degree (or equivalent practical experience) in Animation, Graphic Design, Visual Communication, Multimedia Arts, or a related field.
  • Proficiency in Adobe After Effects, Premiere Pro, and design tools such as Photoshop and Illustrator.
  • A strong portfolio showcasing expertise in motion graphics, animation principles, and visual storytelling.
  • Passion for digital design, animation, and creative problem-solving.
  • Excellent collaboration skills, strong communication abilities, and a growth mindset.
  • Ability to work effectively under tight deadlines in a fast-paced, creative environment.

Nice-to-Haves:

  • Experience with 3D software such as Cinema 4D, Blender, or Maya.
  • Basic knowledge of video editing, compositing, and visual effects.
  • An eye for detail and commitment to producing polished, professional work.

What You’ll Gain

  • Practical, hands-on experience creating motion graphics for real-world brands and campaigns.
  • Mentorship and feedback from experienced designers and creative leads.
  • An opportunity to build a strong, diverse portfolio in motion design and visual storytelling.
  • Access to cutting-edge design tools, techniques, and collaborative projects.
  • Potential for full-time career opportunities based on performance and business needs.
  • A dynamic, supportive, and inspiring work environment where creativity thrives.

Why Nivaro?

At Nivaro, we believe creativity can change the way people experience the world. Our culture celebrates innovation, teamwork, and continuous growth. You’ll be part of a passionate, talented community that encourages pushing creative boundaries and producing meaningful work.

Join Nivaro and craft the digital stories of the future!

How to Apply:

Please submit your resume and cover letter, highlighting your relevant experience and interest in the field of Motion Design or Animation, to careers@wearenivaro.com.

You will be part of a dynamic team where your contributions are valued, and you will have the opportunity to see the direct results of your efforts.

If you are passionate about motion graphics, 3D and visual effects, eager to learn, and ready to make a tangible impact on our social media, we encourage you to apply for this exciting opportunity.
